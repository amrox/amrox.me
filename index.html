Andy Mroczkowski

(amrox) is a software developer living in the San Francisco Bay Area (but from Philly). He is passionate about building great applications for (but not limited to) iOS, Mac and the web.

Andy works with MindSnacks (truly awesome mobile learning games). He also runs Amerzing Industries (software development services). Previously Andy founded PhillyCocoa (Philly Mac and iPhone programming group) and co-founded The Ship It Society (app development collective).

When he's not codering (sic), Andy enjoys cycling (road), disc golf (really), electronic music (everything but trance), inventing new forms of punctuation (see background), vegetarian food (usually), and beer (always).
