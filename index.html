Andy Mroczkowski

(amrox) is a software developer living in Philadelphia, PA (Philly). He is passionate about building applications for (but not limited to) Mac, iPhone and the web.

Andy works for The Neat Company (formerly NeatReceipts) and also organizes PhillyCocoa (Philly Mac and iPhone programming group).

When he's not codering (sic), Andy enjoys cycling (road), disc golf (really), electronic music (everything but trance), inventing new forms of punctuation (see background), vegetarian food (usually), and beer (always).
